RockCo Entertainment Live Stream/Live Performance at Resless Moons Brewery

The band was lucky enough to get a second opportunity to play live in the month of November by making another appearance at Restless Moons Brewery on Friday the 13th. The event was live streamed by an entertainment company created by a group of students enrolled in the Concert Production and Promotion class at James Madison University. The entire 1 hour and 30 minute set can be found on RockCo Entertaintment's YouTube channel.

EP Release Coming Soon!

Bloo Lagoon's long awaited EP is currently in production and the release date will be announced in the near future! In the mean time, be sure to check out their YouTube and SoundCloud pages to hear their latest live recordings.

First Live Performance Since March | Trinity Presbyterian Church

The Trinity Presbyterian Church in Harrisonburg, VA held a small concert on their lawn outside on November 1st for an opportunity to bring some fun and fellowship to their church community since their ability to hold in person services every Sunday has been hindered due to the COVID-19 outbreak this year. Bloo Lagoon was one of three acts to perform, including a young singer/songwriter apart of the congregation as well as the JMU Saxophone Choir.
