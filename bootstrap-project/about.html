About

Biography

Formed originally as a quintet in the summer of 2018, Bloo Lagoon have established themselves as regular entertainers and music makers within the Harrisonburg and James Madison University communities. Fusing funk, rock, and jazz along with the influence of groups such as The Grateful Dead, the band always succeeds at bringing tight, groovy tunes to any event they are apart of. Each member of the now 10-person ensemble is fully immeresed in the JMU School of Music one way or the other, making their songs not only intruiging listens, but incredibly fun to just dance or jam to as well.


Bloo Lagoon early on used to only consist of two guitars, bass, keyboards, and drums, but have since added their own hornline, which includes two trombones and a saxohpone, as well as an auxillary percussionist to beef up the rhythm section for such a large group of players. Balancing 10 different schedules is seldom an easy task, but that doesn't mean the band is incapable of being flexible with it's instrumentation from gig to gig, which can vary from only the most essential elements, to their room shaking, full 10-member potential. Bloo Lagoon is exciting in any capacity, but those special performances when everyone is on board, it is truly a sight to see and a sound to behold.


Bloo Lagoon constantly strives to bring happiness and joy to people through music. Everyhting else simply comes second.
