Self Landscape

My first project and creation as a SMAD major is featured here as what is known as a self landscape. The purpose of this was to act as a visual representation of a short narrative we were required to write that describes ourselves and our life story. One of the first tasks we were required to do in SMAD 201 was to come up with 50 adjectives that described ourselves. Out of those 50 words, we chose 5 that we felt best represented who we are and write a short narrative only based on those adjectives. From there, we needed to choose 5 photos relating to those words, either taken specifically for the project or chosen from our own existing library of photos; either way, they needed to be original images. Ultimately, we compiled the five images based on our personal narrative into one design that told that story by only utilizing imagery. Below is my list of 5 adjectives as well as the narrative that materialized from them. This project was created primarily in Adobe Photoshop.
